About Us

Why we care

Embedded Systems are cool and fun. And more than anything else, easy.

But it's a sad fact that most of the content is not easy to process. Specially for a beginner.

We are a community that strives to spread knowledge.
Our goal is to make our content as easy as possible to understand.

We don't want you to decipher tutorials, guides or books to learn what's in them.
